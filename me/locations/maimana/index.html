AMDEP | 12 Focus Locations

Maimana

Maimana is the capital of north-western Faryab province, and is one of two control locations for the AMDEP research (the other being Narang). Maimana was selected as it is a small-to-medium-size center with established local media. The data to the right is based on 123 respondents from 12 locations around Maimana.
